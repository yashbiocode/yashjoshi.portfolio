About Me

I'm a Biotechnology Specialist from the University of Toronto with interdisciplinary training spanning analytical chemistry, systems biology, bioinformatics, and scientific communication. My work sits at a rare intersection — I can operate in a regulated laboratory environment and translate data outputs into actionable digital strategy.

My focus now is organizations like Sanofi where digital strategy, healthcare data, and innovation converge — where a scientific mindset sharpens analytical rigour, and where I can contribute to meaningful health outcomes at scale.
